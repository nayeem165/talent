About Background Checks

Background checks are initiated by recruiters during the hiring process. Different types of checks may include:

  • Identity Verification - Confirm your identity
  • Criminal Background - Check for criminal records
  • Employment Verification - Verify previous employment
  • Education Verification - Verify educational credentials
  • Credit Check - Financial history check
  • Driving Record - For positions requiring driving
You will be notified before any background check is initiated. All checks are conducted in compliance with applicable laws.
